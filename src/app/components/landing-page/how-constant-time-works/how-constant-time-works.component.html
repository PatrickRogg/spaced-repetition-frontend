How ConstantTime works

ConstantTime is a Flashcard based learning platform that allows you to create and organize your Flashcards. The concepts Spaced Repetition and Active Recall are build into the platform.

Flashcard Question

By trying to answer the question you are using Active Recall. Speak your answer out loud if you can.

Flashcard Answer

Afterwards you check if your answer was correct. If your answer was correct the next time will be further in the future. If your answer was wrong you will see the card on the next day. The only thing you have to worry about is coming back regularly to create and repeat your Flashcards.
